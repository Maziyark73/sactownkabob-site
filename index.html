About Us

At Sactown Kabob, we bring the rich and savory flavors of Persian cuisine to Sacramento. Our dishes are prepared using traditional recipes passed down through generations, with fresh ingredients and love. Whether you're enjoying our juicy kebabs, aromatic rice, or delicious stews, you'll feel like you're dining in Tehran.
