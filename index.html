Welcome to Sactown Kabob

Experience authentic Persian cuisine in Sacramento. Our family-run restaurant brings Persian culinary tradition to Northern California, blending classic flavors with modern hospitality.

Explore Our Specialities

  • Kabobs: Tender skewers of marinated meats grilled to perfection.
  • Healthy Salads: Fresh salads featuring crisp vegetables and flavorful dressings.
  • Stews: Slow-cooked Persian stews like Ghormeh Sabzi and Lamb Shank.

Menu Highlights

  • Zereshk Polo: Saffron chicken served with barberry rice.
  • Lamb Souvlaki: Skewered lamb with grilled vegetables.
  • Chicken Souvlaki: Marinated chicken with aromatic herbs.
  • Joojeh: Grilled chicken breast marinated in saffron and lemon.
  • Beef & Lamb Shish Kabobs: Succulent beef or lamb skewers.
  • Vegetarian Plate: A flavorful assortment of vegetarian delights.
  • Ghormeh Sabzi: Herb stew with beans and tender meat.
  • Lamb Shank: Slow-braised lamb shank with aromatic spices.
  • Appetizers: Hummus, Mast O Khiar (cucumber yogurt), Mast O Moosir (shallot yogurt), Tzatziki.
  • Greek Salad: Mixed greens, tomatoes, cucumbers, olives and feta.

About Us

Sactown Kabob is a labor of love. We source high-quality ingredients and prepare each dish with care. Our mission is to share Persian culture and cuisine with our community through food and hospitality.
